What is Scambaiting?

Scambaiting is when you purposefully call a scam phone number, (like the ones you see on fake tech support popups on the internet,) to mess with them and waste their time. This makes it so less people ara available to take calls and scam people, as they are dealing with you instead! It's a fun hobby, and helps keep scammers from doing what they do, but if it isn't done propperly, it can be dangerous. The scammers can find out information about you, and even install malware and wreck your computer. This site is chock full of useful tools and tips that let you do scambaiting easily and safely.
